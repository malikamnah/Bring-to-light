COUNSELLING

Let’s be honest. Life is sometimes hard, confusing and stressful. Although it is a normal part of life, there are times when the problem seems to reoccur or last longer than is healthy for us. SOMATIC THERAPY

My specialization is in Mindfulness-Based Somatic Therapy. Trauma and stress have a way of becoming locked in our bodies, showing up as undesired symptoms. Somatic therapy takes you on a very personal journey using restorative yoga postures, breathing techniques, mindfulness and resourcing practices, movement and use of “felt sense” to release and heal.

Mindfulness Based-Somatic Therapy (MBST) is a natural body-centred approach. MBST® incorporates Yoga postures, Mindfulness practice, Breathwork, self-compassion, Resourcing practice, Hands-on healing modalities in order to give your body the space and increased capacity to release long term held stress and trauma is a safe way. Our body knows how to heal itself; it wants to heal and is constantly trying. For several reasons this process can become incomplete and “stuck”. This may show up in numerous ways such as reoccurring headaches/migraines, back aches, fibre myalgia, problems with digestion and other chronic symptoms that otherwise seem to have no known cause. Through these various techniques we can help complete this process and bring a sense of relief, freedom and peace.

Hi! I am Amnah!

A Registered Counsellor (RTCc) with ACCT. Certified in Mindfulness-Based Somatic Therapy® as well as level 1 Craniosacral Therapy. I have experience counselling individuals facing grief, anxiety, depression, parenting and relationship concerns, self-esteem, trauma and more. 

Beyond by profession
I was born in Pakistan and raised in Sweden. As an adult, I moved to Canada to start my family. This has given me the wonderful gift of knowledge about different cultures, values, traditions and languages.

Naturally, I have listened to people sharing their stories with great interest and care. This has led me to build a deep understanding and compassion for the human experience that is unique to everyone.

When faced with my own challenges in life I was, with the right support, guidance, self-education, able to find coping skills to see the light out of darkness to support my life.

Today I find it an honour to be able to potentially be that right support to you and help you on your unique and personal journey.

Warmest,
Amnah Malik
