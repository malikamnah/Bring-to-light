COUNSELLING

What is counselling?

Counselling is a process in which we through a therapeutic relationship explore feelings, thoughts, behaviours, patterns and symptoms. This is your unique journey to take as I witness, support, make space for it to emerge. Counselling is not the same as giving advice, but instead though talk therapy and various strategies it allows for awareness, choices and solutions to organically emerge.
